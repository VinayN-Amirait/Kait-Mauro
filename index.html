The Artist

An expert of nothing, a devotee to the mystery

I write when there are words to express what I need to say, and use the visual language I've constructed for myself when there are not.

I'm an abstract painter, a radically self-sharing writer, and a hot mess poetess living in Birmingham, Alabama — surrounded by five dogs, a hoarder-level collection of houseplants, piles of books, and way too many art supplies.

Studio Practice

Amethyst Collection
— currently unfolding

I make paint my way to slow down and work with my body. After many years of always working digitally, the studio practice is my anchor — a slowing-down place bound to me, honest, and lived with.

The Amethyst Collection incorporates inks made from real amethyst stones. I tilt the paper slowly to disturb the inks and create intricate, gold-foiled layers. Each piece is meditative, one-of-a-kind, and deeply personal.
