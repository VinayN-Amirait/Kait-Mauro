The Artist

A radically
self-sharing
creator.

I write when there are words to express what I need to say, and use the visual language I've constructed for myself when there are not.

I'm an abstract painter, a radically self-sharing writer, and a hot mess poetess in Birmingham, Alabama. I live with my five dogs, a hoarder-level collection of houseplants, piles of books, and way too many art supplies.

My current exploration is the Amethyst Collection — abstract pieces incorporating inks made from amethyst stones. I love the meditative quality of making these pieces, tilting the paper slowly and disturbing the inks to create intricate layers of gold-foiled texture.

My artistic practices play a significant role in my healing from complex PTSD. I am my favorite version of myself when working on one of my creative projects.
